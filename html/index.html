IT ALL STARTS WITH YOU.

FOCUSING ON WHAT’S IMPORTANT ...

Newberry is an incredible place to live.

Let’s make it even better. As City Councilwoman, I will actively engage with our community, listen to your needs, and work to shape a government that serves all Newberry residents.

When the community is supported, Newberry prospers.

... NEWBERRY RESIDENTS.

Newberry’s history and traditions reflect its residents. From business owners to government servants, from service workers to educators, Newberry is an interconnection of people and cultures that makes it profoundly unique.

I pledge that I will work tirelessly to strengthen our bonds through good governance – by helping to address the needs of all Newberry citizens.
