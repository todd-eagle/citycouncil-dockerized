Sheena Paige

Living in Newberry since 2004, Sheena has been an active member in her community. While living in Newberry, she came to realize that a large segment of the community lacked adequate transportation to get to and from work.

With this in mind, Sheena created Work Rides, www.workrides.org, a non-profit organization that provides inexpensive travel to and from work places. The service has since expanded to include other destinations like doctors’ appointments, grocery stores, food pantries and childcare businesses.

Sheena has always shown a strong commitment to her community. As city councilwoman, she will show a strong commitment to you.
